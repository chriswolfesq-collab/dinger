      How to Play

      Dinger is a daily baseball guessing game. Your goal is to identify one mystery MLB player from a ladder of clues.

      Your Goal

      Type the player’s name and press Guess. Full names work, and last names work when that last name belongs to only one player in this game.

      Clues

      You start with one clue. There are ten total clues, moving from broad hints to more revealing facts like teams, nicknames, records, jersey numbers, or signature moments.

      Scoring

      • You start with 100 points.
      • Revealing another clue costs 10 points.
      • A wrong guess costs 15 points.
      • Solving earlier gives you a better score.

      Daily Play

      Everyone gets the same puzzle for the day. Once you finish, you can share your result without spoiling the answer.
